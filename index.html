🖨️ Printer Settings

Printing works through your phone's own Print system. When you tap Print, Android shows every printer it knows about — Bluetooth thermal printers (via an app like RawBT), Sunmi's built-in printer, WiFi/USB printers, or "Save as PDF". If your printer doesn't appear, install its print-service app from Play Store once — after that it'll show up automatically here and in every other app.
Paper Width (for receipt layout)
Printing a full page instead? Just choose "A4" / "Fit to page" in the print dialog itself — the same bill works for both.
Invoice Style
A4 style uses your current theme color and font, with a signature line — best for formal invoices or sharing a polished bill on WhatsApp.
